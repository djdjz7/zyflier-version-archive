3.3.1 (Full Pack)

milestone

Version 3.3.0 was a huge leap for our Webshot system.

In this version, we swapped the browser kernel from the Trident, or IE kernel, into the modern Chromium Embedded Framework.(Congratulations, you got another chromium kernel installed on your computer!)

From 3.3.0, we provide full packs for each milestone update, and main packs for important minor updates.

Why we choose 3.3.1 over 3.3.0

We have confirmed that fatal issues exist in 3.3.0 which may cause the whole module to crash.

We fixed the issue in 3.3.1, thus it should be a stable version for daily use.
